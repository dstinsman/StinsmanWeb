Invest Passively in Real Estate

Our investors earn income on real estate projects and renovations without ever picking up a hammer...because they own the financing. Backed by first-position liens and title protections, our private money lending program allows you to earn competitive returns while we handle all the work.

We follow SEC regulatory guidelines for private placements. Under Rule 506(b) of Regulation D, we must establish a substantive relationship with potential investors before presenting specific investment opportunities. This means we need to understand your financial situation, investment experience, and goals before discussing any particular deal.

This educational page helps us begin that process. By learning about our approach and track record, and by reaching out to discuss your investment criteria, we can determine if private lending opportunities might be a good fit for you. Learn more about private placement rules at SEC.gov.

Explore each step of our process below.

1

Build Rapport

You meet our team and review our track record

What we cover: Team introductions, our experience, risk evaluation process, sample case studies, and commitment to transparent communication.

2

Discuss Lending Options

We Review rates, terms, LTV, and security

Clear presentation of terms, sample returns, interest accrual, first-position lien protection, title company escrow, and hazard insurance requirements.

3

PML Commitment

Investor agrees to terms for private money lending

Commitment includes determining minimum loan amount and interest rate, expected hold period, fund acceptance process (wire to title), and documentation details.

4

Deal Identified

We find a property that matches our criteria

Deal packet includes After Repair Value, purchase price, rehab estimate, timeline, exit plan, and conservative pro forma showing lender protections.

5

PML Selected

Match deal to available investor

Selected lender reviews deal packet, confirms funding, and we coordinate timing for wiring and loan document preparation.

6

Loan Docs Prepared

Note, mortgage, title instructions

Documents drafted with clear terms, lender protections (first lien), and wiring instructions. Samples provided for review before closing.

7

Funds Wired to Title

Direct to escrow, through title company

Wiring to title ensures neutral third-party control. Title disburses per settlement statement so investor principal is properly secured.

8

Closing

Mortgage recorded, interest starts accruing

Investor mortgage is recorded, settlement statements show distribution, and we immediately begin renovation per project timeline.

9

Renovation Process

Weekly updates and tracking

Scheduled photo and change-order updates, budget tracking, and optional milestone inspections with frequent communication.

10

Sale or Refinance

Exit strategy executed

Property is either sold or refinanced to return capital to the investor. Lender principal and accrued interest are returned per loan terms.

11

Funds Returned + Interest

Principal and interest wired to investor

Final wires issued by title at closing or by refinancing lender. We provide final accounting and PDF settlement statement for your records.

12

Repeat

Cycle through the process again

Upon completion of a successful project, you have the option to re-invest your capital into the next leg of our journey.
