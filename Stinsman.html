About Us

We are the Stinsmans, a husband-and-wife team dedicated to transforming properties in the Tampa Bay area and beyond. With a combined background in technology and real estate operations, we specialize in revitalizing homes through strategic renovations and smart investing. Our mission is simple; to create lasting value for homeowners and our community.

Our Work

From flipping houses to building long-term rental portfolios, we approach each property with a focus on quality, sustainability, and attention to detail. Whether it’s breathing new life into a neglected home or creating passive income opportunities for our lending partners, we believe every home tells a story - a narrative in bricks and mortar. Our Partners

We partner with private lenders to fund real estate investments secured by tangible assets. You fund the deal, we secure the asset and execute the plan. Our approach is straightforward:

  • We identify and secure the opportunity
  • We manage the renovation or stabilization
  • We execute a clear exit strategy

Our partners benefit from structured, asset-backed investments while we handle the day-to-day execution. We do the heavy lifting and our lenders enjoy returns secured by real estate assets, with clearly defined terms and exit strategies.
